Common Questions

Before you reach out,
here are quick answers.

The questions we hear most often — answered honestly and simply.

How long does it take to receive my completed SOP?
Standard delivery for an SOP is 3–5 business days from the date of consultation. If you have a pressing deadline, express delivery in 24–48 hours is available at an additional fee. We've never missed a deadline.
Is the free consultation really free? What does it include?
Yes — absolutely free, no credit card, no catch. In the consultation (usually via WhatsApp, email, or a brief call), we review your profile, discuss your target programs, and explain exactly how we'd approach your application. You only pay if you decide to proceed.
Do you write for all countries and universities?
Yes. We've helped students apply to universities in the USA, UK, Canada, Australia, Germany, Japan, South Korea, New Zealand, and across Europe. Our writers are trained in country-specific formats — a German motivation letter and a US SOP are very different documents, and we know both.
How many revisions are included?
All our packages include unlimited revisions until you're satisfied. We work with you iteratively — if you want tone changes, structural edits, or new content added, we'll do it. Your confidence in the final document is the measure of our success.
Will my SOP be original? Can I get a plagiarism report?
Every document is written from scratch — no templates, no recycled content. We run a plagiarism check on all final documents and can provide a report on request. We also do not use AI-generated text; every word is written by a human writer.
Can you help with visa SOPs and dependent letters?
Yes — visa SOPs are one of our specialized services. We write student visa SOPs, visit visa letters, and dependent/escort SOPs for spouses, parents, and children. Our visa SOPs are structured to pre-empt common refusal reasons and reflect country-specific immigration formatting.
