How It All Began

From one student's
struggle to a global mission.

In 2016, Arjun Sharma — then a final-year engineering student in Kathmandu — spent three months writing and rewriting his SOP for graduate programs in the United States. He had a strong academic record, solid research experience, and a clear vision. But when rejections came, he realized something most students never get told: the quality of your story matters as much as your grades.

After eventually getting accepted to a master's program in Canada — and helping two classmates rewrite their applications afterward — Arjun saw a pattern. Students from South Asia, Southeast Asia, and developing regions were consistently underselling themselves in their written documents, not because they lacked merit, but because no one had taught them how to advocate for themselves in a Western academic context.

SOPmandu was born in 2017 from a tiny apartment in Kathmandu with a laptop, a shared Google Doc, and a deep conviction: every student who works hard enough to dream internationally deserves writing support that meets their ambition.

What began as helping a handful of friends evolved into a trusted service that has since guided over two thousand students to universities across 30+ countries — and we're only getting started.

Our Principles

The values we
never compromise on.

Every interaction, every document, every revision is guided by these five principles. They are not aspirational posters on a wall — they are the daily decisions we make for every student we serve.

Value 01

Student-First Approach

Every decision starts with one question: what is best for this student? We put your goals, timeline, and emotional wellbeing above everything else. You are never just a transaction to us.

Value 02

Precision & Quality

Good enough is never good enough. Every document undergoes multiple editorial passes, peer review, and plagiarism screening before delivery. We sweat the small details because admissions committees do too.

Value 03

Integrity & Honesty

We will never tell you your application is stronger than it is. We will always give you honest feedback — even when it's hard to hear. Honest guidance now saves you from disappointment later.

Value 04

Deep Personalization

We do not use templates. Ever. We do not recycle phrases. Every SOP we write is rooted in your specific story, your specific program, and your specific aspirations. It sounds like you — just your best self.

Value 05

Commitment to Success

We don't stop at "delivered." We stay with you through revisions, last-minute changes, and new applications. Your success is not a deliverable — it's an ongoing commitment that we take personally.
